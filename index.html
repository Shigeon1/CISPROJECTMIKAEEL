AI in Education

Artificial Intelligence is transforming teaching and learning by teachers and students. ChatGPT and adaptive learning software learn dynamically according to a student's requirement and offer a chance to a student to study at his pace. AI also displaces the teachers from their position using auto-grading and tutoring.

AI Education Concept

Personalized learning, access, real-time feedback: Benefits.

Technology dependence, threat of data privacy: Drawbacks.

AI in Medicine

AI in medicine allows doctors to conduct robotic surgery, instant patient information, and faster diagnosis. One example of one of the devices that aid in conducting more precise and safer surgery is the Da Vinci Surgical Robot. AI also aids in diagnosing illnesses like cancer at an early stage by interpreting medical scans.

Da Vinci Surgical Robot

Advantages: More precise surgery, faster diagnosis, better outcomes.

Downsides: Pricy, training required, chance of technical breakdown.
